ABOUT ME

I’m Dominic B. Geraga, a 22-year-old
traditional artist and multimedia student
based in Pagadian City, Zamboanga del Sur,
Philippines. I specialize in charcoal,
graphite, and mixed media art, with a
focus on creating detailed portraits and
nature-inspired works.

EDUCATION

Currently, I’m pursuing my degree in
Multimedia Studies at Northwestern
Mindanao State University. My academic
background complements my artistic
endeavors, helping me explore new ways to
merge traditional art with modern
techniques and tools.

SKILLS AND EXPERTISE

• Charcoal and graphite art techniques
• Mixed media creations on toned paper
• Proficiency in using high-quality
materials like Strathmore paper and
Faber-Castell tools
• Realistic and expressive portrait art

ARTISTIC JOURNEY

Since 2015, I’ve been dedicated to honing
my craft, producing artwork that tells
stories and evokes emotion. My portfolio
showcases a mix of commissioned pieces and
personal projects, each reflecting my
passion for artistry.
