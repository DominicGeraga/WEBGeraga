• Size: 9x12 inches

Delivery and Shipping

For Pagadian City Clients:

• I offer meet-ups or delivery.
• If unavailable, I can personally deliver the artwork to you.

Outside Pagadian City:

• Customers outside Pagadian City will be responsible for the shipping fees.

Payment Options:

• GCash: Convenient and secure mobile payments.
• Cash: Available for meet-ups or deliveries.
• A 50% downpayment is required for all orders, with the balance due before delivery.

How to Order

1. Browse the gallery and decide on the artwork or custom commission you'd like to order.
2. Contact me.
3. Provide clear and high-quality reference photos. Blurry or low-resolution references are not accepted to
ensure the best outcome for your artwork.
4. Place your order at least several days before the artwork is needed. Rushed commissions are not accepted 
to maintain the quality of the artwork.
5. Finalize the details, including size, frame options, and delivery preferences.
6. Pay a 50% downpayment to confirm your order. The remaining balance must be settled before delivery or 
upon delivery for meet-ups within Pagadian City.
